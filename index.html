BronxConnect AI

Problem Description

Many people in the Bronx struggle to find important support services such as housing, food assistance, and mental health resources. Information is often spread across multiple websites, offices, and organizations, which makes it difficult to access quickly. This can be overwhelming for people already dealing with stress, financial hardship, or family problems. As a result, many individuals experience delays in getting the help they need or may not get help at all.

AI Solution

BronxConnect AI is an intelligent support system designed to connect users with the right community services quickly and efficiently. The system asks users simple questions about their needs and uses artificial intelligence to provide fast and relevant recommendations. Instead of searching through different sources, users can find support in one place. This saves time, reduces confusion, and makes it easier for people to get connected to the help they need.

Implementation Plan

This solution can be built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the website interface. AI tools such as chatbots or recommendation systems can be used to process user input and match it with available services. A database would store information about local resources, including shelters, food programs, mental health support, and emergency assistance. The AI would review the user’s responses and provide the most appropriate recommendations based on their needs.

Expected Impact

BronxConnect AI can improve access to essential services by making information easier to find. It reduces the time users spend searching for help and lowers stress during difficult situations. It can also help organizations operate more efficiently by guiding people to the right resources faster. Compared with traditional methods, this solution offers a more organized, quicker, and more user-friendly way to connect people with support.

Ethics and Challenges

One major ethical concern is privacy. Since users may share sensitive personal information, the system must protect data and ensure it is not misused. Another challenge is avoiding bias in AI recommendations so that all users receive fair and accurate results. It is also important to keep service information updated because outdated recommendations could send users to the wrong place or delay needed support.

Reflection

This project helped me understand how artificial intelligence can be applied to real-world problems, especially in my community. I learned how to organize ideas into a website and explain how AI can be used in a practical way to improve access to important services. One challenge was thinking through how the system would realistically work, but doing that helped me better understand both the possibilities and limitations of AI.
