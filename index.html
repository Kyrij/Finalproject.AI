BronxConnect AI

Problem Description

Many people in the Bronx struggle to find important support services such as housing, food assistance, and mental health resources. Information is often spread across different websites or offices, making it difficult to access. This can be overwhelming, especially for people already dealing with stressful situations. As a result, many individuals experience delays in getting the help they need or may not get help at all.

AI Solution

BronxConnect AI is an intelligent system designed to connect users with the right services quickly and efficiently. The system asks users simple questions about their needs and uses artificial intelligence to provide fast and relevant recommendations. Instead of searching through multiple sources, users can find support in one place, saving time and reducing stress.

Implementation Plan

This solution can be built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the website interface. AI tools such as chatbots or recommendation systems can be used to process user input. A database would store information about local services, including shelters, food programs, and mental health resources. The system would match user responses with the most appropriate services based on their needs.

Impact

BronxConnect AI can significantly improve access to essential services by making information easier to find. It reduces the time users spend searching for help and lowers stress during difficult situations. It also benefits organizations by directing people to the right resources more efficiently, improving overall service delivery.

Ethics & Challenges

One major ethical concern is user privacy. Since users may share sensitive personal information, the system must protect data and ensure it is not misused. Another challenge is avoiding bias in AI recommendations, making sure all users receive fair and accurate results. Keeping the service information updated is also important to avoid sending users to incorrect or outdated resources.

Reflection

This project helped me understand how artificial intelligence can be applied to real-world problems, especially in my community. I learned how to organize ideas into a structured website and how AI can improve access to important services. One challenge was figuring out how to make the system realistic, but it helped me think more critically about how technology can be used to support people.
